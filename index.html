Our Approach

We cook with one heat source: fire. No gas line runs through this kitchen — only oak, almond wood, and the patience to let embers do the work.

Every dish passes over the coals before it reaches the pass, whether that's a whole fish, a head of charred lettuce, or the bread that opens the meal. Smoke is a seasoning here, not an accident.

We built the menu around what the fire changes about an ingredient, not around what it can disguise. Simple plates, few components, nothing between you and what came off the grill.
